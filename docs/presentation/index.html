The problem

Organizing game nights shouldn't feel like homework.

Manual WhatsApp signups, spreadsheets for draws, missing context on who played with whom — it doesn't scale, and it's hard to keep fair.

Chat-based RSVPs

Tracking "IN"/"OUT" messages across threads — no history, no waitlist, no fairness.

Spreadsheet draws

Pairing is improvised, same partners repeat week after week, and balance is guesswork.

No ranking signal

Without a ranking system, tiers feel arbitrary and the community can't track progress.

The solution

One app for the whole club.
From sign-up to scoreboard.

A modern, mobile-first web app built on the stack you already trust. Role-based access for players and admins.

Smart RSVPs

Capacity + waitlist + auto-promotion.

Seeded draws

Balanced, reproducible, tier-aware.

Live rankings

5-week rolling rating per player.

Fair results

Transparent scoring, auto ratings.

Feature 01 · RSVP

First-come, first-served — fair by design.

Players register for events with enforced capacity. When someone cancels, the next waitlisted player is promoted automatically and notified by email.

  • Capacity limits with automatic waitlist ordering.
  • Auto-promotion when a confirmed player drops out.
  • RSVP cutoff enforcement with admin override.
  • Email notifications for confirmations and promotions.
Event detail

Everything about a night — one tap away.

Players see confirmed roster, tier time slots, and their own rating. Once the draw publishes, the same screen shows courts, partners, and opponents.

  • Tier time slots clearly signposted (Masters 7PM, Explorers 8:30PM).
  • Live capacity and waitlist position for everyone.
  • Three tabs: Details, Draw, Results — the full lifecycle in one page.
Event lifecycle

A clear state machine from draft to published.

Every event flows through a small set of well-defined states. The UI adapts per state, and admins can freeze the roster early when it helps — or skip straight to the draw.

Draft → Open → Frozen → Drawn → Published

Draft → Open

RSVPs open at the scheduled time. Players register and fill the waitlist.

Freeze (optional)

Admins can lock the roster early — useful for late cancellations. Draws can also run directly from Open.

Drawn → Published

Seeded draw runs, results get entered, rankings recompute, notifications go out.

Feature 02 · Draws

Seeded algorithm. Balanced matchups.

Every draw uses a deterministic seed for reproducibility, avoids recent partners and opponents, and assigns players to Masters or Explorers tier based on rating.

  • Seeded & reproducible — same seed, same draw, always.
  • Avoids repeats from the last 4 sessions.
  • Tier-based courts & times (e.g. Masters 7PM, Explorers 8:30PM).
  • Round-robin scheduling with independent round numbering per tier.

Feature 03 · Results

Transparent scoring, auto-computed ratings.

Admins enter set scores per match. The system validates, saves, and recomputes every player's weekly score and rating in one click.

Masters match

300 + 20 × sets won

Loser earns 20 × sets won. Ties: per-set points only.

Explorers match

200 + 15 × sets won

Same structure, tier-scaled rewards.

Feature 04 · Rankings

A fair rating that rewards consistency.

Scores are split per team and averaged per round played that week. Your rating is the simple average of your last 5 weeks with activity.

  • Dynamic tiers per event — assigned from current ratings, not sticky.
  • Weekly aggregation — your week is the average of rounds played.
  • Rolling 5-week rating — recovery from a bad week stays possible.
  • Podium + full leaderboard with trend indicators.

Players, venues, and courts — all in one place.

Browse the roster, open any player's profile, and manage venues with their courts. Everything auto-links with events and draws.

Personal

Every player gets their own story.

A profile page shows current rating, last 5 weeks of activity, recent matches and partners, and the trend line that led to today's number.

  • Rating history and trend visualization.
  • Match history with partners and opponents.
  • Editable preferences, avatar, notifications.
Experience

Native feel, zero install.

Installable PWA with offline fallback, safe-area support for notched devices, and polished touch targets. Works great on iPhone, Android, and desktop.

  • Add to Home Screen — full-screen standalone mode.
  • Offline page + pre-cached assets.
  • Safe-area insets honored on iOS notched devices.
  • Dark mode + WCAG 2.2 AA contrast throughout.
  • i18n-ready — English & Portuguese via [lang] routes.
Access control

Two roles. Clear boundaries.

Simple access model — players RSVP and follow their progress, admins run the club. Permissions are enforced end-to-end: UI, API, and database.

Role · Viewer
Players
The default role for every member of the club.
  • Register for events & manage their own RSVPs
  • View draws, courts, partners, and opponents
  • See match results once published
  • Check the leaderboard and personal rating history
  • Edit their own profile and preferences
Role · Admin
Club admins
Full control over events, players, and club configuration.
  • Create, freeze, and publish events
  • Generate draws & override the roster when needed
  • Enter match results and recompute rankings
  • Manage users, invitations, venues & courts
  • Configure tier rules, scoring, and email templates
Role-based UI

Two views. Same app.

The UI adapts to who's looking. Players get a focused roster view; admins get management controls on the same screens — all enforced server-side.
